Smoothie

Smoothies are a great healthy treat and can be deliciousat the same time. Easy to make and take on the go as well!

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Banana
  • Spinach
  • Peanut Butter
  • Chia Seeds

Steps

  1. Chop banana and strawberries to remove stems
  2. Scoop peanut butter into measuring cup (about 1/2 cup)
  3. Add fruit, peanut butter and chia seeds to blender
  4. Blend on high for 1 minute or until desired consistency is met
  5. Pour into cup and add garnish
