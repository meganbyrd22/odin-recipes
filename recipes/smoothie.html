Smoothie

Smoothies are a great healthy treat and can be deliciousat the same time. Easy to make and take on the go as well!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Cheese(optional)

Steps

  1. Bring 6 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add salt and olive oil to taste
  3. Add noodles and cook for 8-12 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Strain noodles in collandar
  5. Place noodles in bowl and add butter
  6. Top with cheese if desired
