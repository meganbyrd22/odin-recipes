Funfetti

Easy to make cake tomake your birthday special!

Funfetti cake is perfect for any occasion andany age. It's fun, colorful and delicious! You can't go wrong with this easy and yummy recipe. Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Funfetti mix
  • Vegetable oil
  • Water
  • One medium egg
  • Frosting

Steps

  1. Place funfetti mix in a mixing bowl
  2. Add oil, water and egg
  3. Beat together by hand or with mixer
  4. Grease pan and add mixture to pan
  5. Bake in 350 degree oven for 25 to 35 minutes or until golden brown
  6. Let cool before frosting
